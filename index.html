About Me

At my core I am a problem solver, a communicator and an entrepreneur. I believe that there is nothing impossible when we set our minds on it.

After 7 years of building a business and career in real estate, during which I received multiple awards, I am taking 2020 to reset and recharge by expanding my skills into Product Management, taking a focused course at Product School (San Francisco), and picking up some coding, problem-solving, creative skills and programming expertise via the Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp at University of Utah.

Having lived in multiple countries (China, U.K. and U.S.) and having experience with both the business and engineering world, I am able to work effectively with people from diverse backgrounds.

I enjoy working with and love to learn from great people who want to help others and improve the world. So if that's you, reach out and say Hi!
